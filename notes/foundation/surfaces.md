# [Surfaces](https://material.io/design/environment/surfaces.html)
1. varying x & y dimensions, fixed z dimension
2. elevation (change in z index) creates shadows
3. infinite resolution
4. content is contained inside a surface
5. a solid that could change in shape, opacity and x,y sizes
6. can move along any axis, but
> motion along the z-axis is typically a result of user interaction
7. three types of behavior - rigid, stretchable and pannable
8. can be transparent, semi-transparent, or opaque
9. apply scrims to make less prominent
